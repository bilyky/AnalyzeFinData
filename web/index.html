🛡️ Project AETHER Overview

Project AETHER (Automated Equity Trading & Heuristic Evaluation Routine) is a disciplined quantitative risk and capital allocation engine designed for systematic equity evaluation, portfolio rotation, and capital preservation.

REGIME-ADAPTIVE Sizing & stops auto-scale
ZERO-TRUST Loud warnings & safety gates
AUTONOMIC HEALER Proactive 24/7 session keeper

📊 1. Quantitative Models & Scoring Engines

`buying_ratio` (BR) Model (`powergauge.py`)

Compiles relative volume, Money Flow, oscillators, and weekly seasonality into a unified score in [-10.0, +10.0]. Sourced from Marc Chaikin's PowerGauge Rating (PGR) methodology.

`S10` Short-Term Entry Score (`scoring.py`)

Standardizes short-term entry quality over a 10-day trading horizon. Aggregates Relative Volume, Money Flow, contrarian Industry Strength, and technical pattern overlays.

`L60` Long-Term Trend Score (`scoring.py`)

Measures intermediate-to-long-term trend strength and durability over a rolling 60-day horizon, focusing on core moving average alignments.

Weekly Seasonality Detection Engine

Groups 25 years of historical closes by month and week-of-month, calculating the historical 10-day forward return of that calendar week to apply weighted tailwinds or headwinds.

Fibonacci Channels & RSI Divergences

Maps price relative to Fibonacci retracement levels (23.6%, 38.2%, 50%, 61.8%) and tracks price-RSI(14) momentum divergences. Based on J. Welles Wilder Jr.'s RSI and classical Fibonacci standards.

🔍 2. Advanced Pattern Recognition Suite

Japanese Candlestick Recognition

Aggregates 17 candlestick patterns over a rolling 5-day lookback window. Based on Munehisa Homma's original Japanese rice-trading methodology.

Classical Chart Formations

Identifies structural technical formations including Head & Shoulders, Double Tops/Bottoms, Cup & Handle, and Bull/Bear Flags.

Trend Momentum Crossovers

Monitors moving average crossings (Golden/Death Crosses) and MACD crossings (signal line and trend crossovers) to lock in entry direction.

Contrarian Calibration Override

Programmatically negates the combined pattern score (-1.0 * score) because backtests prove overbought patterns act as contrarian, prioritizing oversold bottoming plays.

🛡️ 3. Risk Management & Exit Heuristics

Regime-Adaptive Strategy Profiles

Auto-scales risk based on market trend: Defensive (max 3 slots, 10% size, 50% cash), Balanced (max 5 slots, 15% size, 20% cash), and Aggressive (max 6 slots, 15% size, 0% cash).

Price Slope Acceleration Trigger

Computes price slope derivatives to verify that downward selling velocity has flattened and turned positive. Sourced from Victor Sperandeo's (Trader Vic) "1-2-3 Reversal" and "2B Pattern" bottom-confirmation concepts.

S&P Market Breadth Filter

Monitors the score gap between Cap-Weighted SPY and Equal-Weighted RSP. If delta > 4.0, it flags a technology-concentrated, narrow market top and automatically downgrades the active risk profile. Based on John Bollinger's concepts of volatility and index breadth.

2.5-Sigma Bubble Guard

Calculates standard deviation distance from the 500-day mean. If Z-score > 2.5, the stock is blacklisted to avoid deploying cash at overextended bubble peaks. Inspired by John Bollinger's standard-deviation volatility band concepts.

Unified Exit Policy & Flower Protection

Hard ATR stop-loss floor always overrides soft signals. Based on Peter Lynch's 'Flower Protection' trailing stops concept where soft exits on profitable, above-50-DMA positions are downgraded to REVIEW to let winners run.

Catastrophic Gap Guard (CNXC Trap)

Instantly rejects any buy order if today's price is more than 8% below yesterday's workbook close, protecting capital from waterfall crashes on earnings panics.

Antifragile Safety Gates

Employs zero-trust risk safety gates. Logs a 'Failure DNA' ledger of closed losses, programmatically adjusting model coefficients over future runs to prevent repeating errors. Based on Nassim Nicholas Taleb's concepts of antifragility.

Secular Scarcity Allocations

Enforces dedicated capital share allocations strictly to metals, agriculture, and grid utility assets. Sourced from Jeremy Grantham's concepts of hard asset secular scarcity supercycles.

Systematic Sell & Decision Quality Evaluation (`decision_eval.py`)

Evaluates exits with a hybrid qualitative rubric combining fundamental risks with charts. Automatically logs, scores, and audits decision quality for both closed trades and active reviews.

🧱 4. System Resilience & Headless Safety Gates

System Watchdog & Healer

Headless background self-healing traceback circuit-breaker. Intercepts script crashes, patches code defects, and restarts tasks safely.

Proactive E*TRADE Session Keeper

Silent autonomic background keeper running hourly (PT1H). Calls the E*TRADE OAuth renewal endpoint in the background, keeping the session active 24/7.

Windows UTF-8 Hardening

Employs a custom SafeStreamWrapper and OS-level environmental encoding to prevent Windows cp1252 background console crashes.

Content Validation Gates

RapidAPI and E*TRADE parsers verify data structures before saving, instantly aborting if rate limits are detected to protect multi-year historical files.

🔌 5. Production & Brokerage Integrations

Real-Account Shadow Copilot

Logs into your real-money production E*TRADE accounts headlessly, retrieves 44 active positions, cross-references local sheets, and emails styled HTML buy/sell tickets.

Loud Symbology Exception Warnings

Unpriced watchlist symbols log severe, uppercase SYMBOLOGY ERROR, ensuring the watchdog alerts you while keeping stop checks active for other positions.

Gaps Quote Fallback Scraper

If E*TRADE returns partial/empty quotes, the system seamlessly scrapes Google Finance live price elements (span jsname="Pdsbrc") to fill the gaps.

NYSEARCA ETF Exchange Suffixing

Suffixes international ETFs with NYSEARCA, NASDAQ, NYSE, and AMEX to guarantee complete pricing redundancy when API servers are offline.
